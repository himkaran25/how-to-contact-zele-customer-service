How to Connect With Zelle® Support

A simple guide to getting help the right way

Zelle® is a popular digital payment service that allows users to send and receive money directly through their bank. If you experience issues such as failed transfers, incorrect recipients, or account access problems, it’s important to contact support through **official and secure channels**.

1. Contact Your Bank or Credit Union

Zelle® support is primarily handled by the bank or credit union connected to your account. Since Zelle is built into many banking apps, your financial institution has access to your transaction details.

  • Open your bank’s mobile app or website
  • Look for “Zelle®” under Payments or Transfers
  • Use your bank’s customer support options

2. Use the Official Zelle® App (If Applicable)

If you enrolled using the standalone Zelle® app (not through a bank), you can access support directly within the app.

  • Open the Zelle® app
  • Go to the Help or Support section
  • Follow the on-screen guidance

3. Visit the Official Zelle® Help Center

Zelle® provides an official online help center with answers to common questions about payments, limits, and security.

Important Safety Tip:
Zelle® does not offer unsolicited phone calls or social media support. Be cautious of third parties claiming to be “Zelle support.”

4. Protect Yourself From Scams

  • Never share one-time passcodes or login credentials
  • Only send money to people you trust
  • Use official bank or app channels for help
